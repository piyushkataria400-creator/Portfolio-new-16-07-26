Performance Commitment

Qualified Enquiry Guarantee

My singular focus is generating enquiries that have genuine interest in your product or service. This is the commitment I make to eligible clients.

My Focus Is Not Vanity Metrics

Impressions, clicks, and reach are not business outcomes. Qualified enquiries are.

🛡️
Performance
Guarantee

I do not run campaigns to inflate reporting dashboards or impress with reach numbers. Every campaign is built around your sales pipeline, your ideal client profile, and the types of enquiries your team can realistically convert into paying customers.

I measure success the same way you do: qualified business enquiries from people with genuine interest — not impressions, clicks, or cost per click metrics that mean nothing to your bottom line.

For eligible clients who meet the required advertising budget, sales process, and follow-up requirements:

If I am unable to generate a minimum agreed number of qualified enquiries within the campaign period, I will continue optimising and working on the account at no additional management fee until the agreed target is achieved.

This is not a promotional promise. It is a professional commitment rooted in confidence in my process — and in the quality of clients I choose to work with. I only accept engagements where I genuinely believe I can deliver.

Disclaimer & Eligibility: The Qualified Enquiry Guarantee applies only to clients meeting all qualifying criteria including: minimum monthly advertising budget as agreed at onboarding; a proven sales process with a qualified team capable of following up enquiries within business hours; a competitive service offering appropriate for the target market; and adherence to the recommended follow-up process. Results are influenced by industry competitiveness, seasonal demand, offer quality, and follow-up speed. The guarantee covers continued management fee waiver during an extended optimisation period and does not constitute a monetary refund. Full guarantee terms are agreed in writing prior to engagement commencement.
